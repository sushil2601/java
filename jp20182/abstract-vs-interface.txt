Abstract Class
--------------
1. Data member may or may not be final
2. Member functions may or may not be abstract.
3. Member functions are NOT by default public
4. inherited by other classes using "extends" keyword
5. Multiple inheritance is not supported by abstract classes
6. may or may not have constructors

Interface
---------
1. Data members are always final
2. Member functions are always abstract.
3. member functions are by default public
4. inherited by other classes using "implements" keyword
5. Multiple inheritance is supported by interfaces
6. Can never have constructors
